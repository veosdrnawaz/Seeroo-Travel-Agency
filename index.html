How to Prepare For Your Trip

To ensure your short mountain escape is comfortable, we suggest packing the following essentials:

  • Jacket/Shawl (Shogran gets cold in the evening)
  • Sturdy jogging shoes or grippy sneakers
  • Original CNIC card (Mandatory for verification)
  • Water bottle & basic personal medicines
  • Umbrella (unpredictable mountain showers)
  • Mobile power bank for long photography sessions
